Guitar Gallery

I think I began collecting guitars the first time I picked one up!  The collection below is made up of instruments I currently own, with the exception of a few instruments which have put out to pasture or been sold. In addition, although I will never be competition for Martin, Gibson, or PRS, some of them I am proud to say I built myself. Although I am primarily an acoustic player, I do have a couple of interesting electric guitars and basses.  These days I rotate the few I keep at home to play, and the rest are stored in several climate controlled locations, keeping them safe in the case of water or fire damage.  Mouse over and enjoy!
