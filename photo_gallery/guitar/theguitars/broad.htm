Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Harmony
Model#: Broadway
Year: 1973
Description: This guitar was given to me by my Mother-in-law, and came with the original softshell case.  She bought it new in the 1973.   This guitar appeared in Vintage Guitar Magazine in a picture with my other three Harmony guitars called "Four Part Harmony."   All of my Harmonys were made in the USA, and with the exception of the Stella, they are solid wood instruments and include a steel (non-adjustable) rod in the neck.
