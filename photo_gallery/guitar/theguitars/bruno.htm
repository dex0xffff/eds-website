Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Bruno
Model#: Glee Club Four-String Tenor Guitar
Year: 1925
Description: This was purchased at a Maryland vintage guitar show my son and I attended as a Christmas gift for my wife. When I spotted it, it clearly needed tons of work to bring it back to life, but being a four string tenor guitar, (or depending on the tuning, baritone uke) with nylon strings I thought it would be perfect for her.  It was missing one tuning key, only had one string attached to it, and the bridge was too short to do the job.  It had so much charm that I couldn't pass it up. I love the beautiful tailpiece which is as functional as it is unique (check out the photo below). The sticker in the sound hole is very colorful, but unfortunately, doesn't give much information.  I have only seen one other guitar like this one and that was in an article in Acoustic Guitar Magazine about two street musician's in New York.  It has a great tone and just begs to be played! The seller said it was made about 1925.
