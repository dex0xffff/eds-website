Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Cortez
Model#: LP22BC
Year: 1976
Description: This is a Japanese-made copy modeled after a Gibson Les Paul model.  It was purchased new from the Music House. This was another guitar I modified.  The original tuning keys were replaced by Gibson/Kluson copies made by the Saga Company.  I fashioned a new pickguard, toggle switch knob, and truss rod cover from genuine Walnut.  The guitar was first sold to a friend and later to my friend and musical partner, Steve McCoy.  Recently Steve gave the guitar to his son, Sean, after I cleaned it up, made several minor adjustments to improve the playability, and replaced the broken output jack.
