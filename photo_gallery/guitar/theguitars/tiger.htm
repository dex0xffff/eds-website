Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Emenee Toy Company
Model#: Tiger Guitar and amp
Year: 1965
Description: This instrument was purchased new by my parents.  Although produced by a toy company, it was modeled after the Maccaferri guitars made in the previous decade.  I consider it to be my first guitar.  I owned and played it for two years before it was broken by a boy on Halloween night. It was slung over my back as part of my costume.  He threw an egg at me and hit the guitar in a weak spot, cracking the front and collapsing the bridge.  Emenee also offered a sunburst model, and although totally made of plastic, mine was molded to resemble a dark, rich wood. The matching amp was a small, battery operated portable unit with a shoulder strap.  The "pickup" was a small contact mic with a thin metal "foot" that slide under the treble side of the bridge.
