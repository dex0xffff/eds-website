Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Univox
Model#: ES-335 copy
Year: 1971
Description: This instrument was purchased new by my parents from the Music House, was basically a Gibson 335 copy, and was in fact my first electric guitar. It had no logo on the headstock. Univox parent company at the time was Marshall, of British amplifier fame, but this guitar was Asian made.  The guitar pictured here is not the one I owned as I can't find an image of the instrument alone... however this is a close approximation of how it looked, with the exception that my guitar was a red to black sunburst.  The other pictures included here are of me holding the original guitar.  This instrument was disposed of in 1978 after I had modified it several times.  Although the final product looked good, with a multitude of switches and decorations, it was pretty much unplayable.
