Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Taylor
Model#: Baby, 3/4 size travel guitar
Year: 2002
Description: I received this as a gift from my wife. I had been talking about getting one at some point for travel and she surprised me with it as a thank you for my time spent renovating her kitchen. I have taken it on car trips as well as on a long train trip, as it is the perfect size for travel.  Since receiving this instrument, we have purchased two more of the exact same model, one for each of our sons. It has a really solid feel and an incredible sound for such a small sound box.  Notable to this instrument is it's solid mahogany top and removable neck. It also came with an incredible gig bag with pockets and a shoulder strap.
