Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Vega
Model#: 8 string Banjolin
Year: Unknown
Description: This instrument was another bequeathed to me by Bruce Hathorne.  The headstock had been refinished and a abalone cross inlayed into it.  Other than that it appears to be all original.  There was an old crack where the headstock joins the neck, which opened up and I had professionally repaired by Raymond Guitars.  Currently, it needs to be refinished on the face of the headstock. It is a fun sounding instrument and inspired an original song: "The Only Lonely Sea," which my friend Patrick and I still play often, him on guitar and me on the banjolin.  It came with the original shaped, plush-lined hardshell case.
