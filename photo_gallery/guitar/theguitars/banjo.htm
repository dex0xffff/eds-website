Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Washburn
Model#: Custom 5-String
Year: Unknown
Description: This instrument was one of five bequeathed to me by my friend Bruce Hathorne in 1993. The brand name is Washburn and there is no other model designation except the word "Custom" appears on the truss rod cover.  I have never been able to find any reference to this model anywhere, in print or online, but I will keep trying.  My guess is that it was made in the mid-seventies.  It is in excellent shape and is all original with the exception of the armrest, which I made myself from ebony and binding.  I do have the original armrest, but the one I made went with a six string banjo neck that I made and installed, but then didn't like! The back of the resonator is curly maple.  The inlay is really beautiful and let me tell you ...IT IS LOUD!  It came to me with the original hardshell case.
