Guitar Gallery

 

Company: Ovation
Model#: Custom Balladeer #1112
Year: 1978
Description: I purchased this instrument new from the Music House.  It is still all original and in excellent condition, with the exception of a few small cracks in the plastic soundhole ring.  An original Bill Lawrence soundhole pickup powered this guitar for stage use for many years before it was retired and replaced on stage with a Guild JF30.  I still own this guitar and the original Ovation hardshell case. The picture below of my daughter Stephanie helping me with a bass note was taken when she was less than a year old, she is 26 now!  I was using this guitar to record a slide part for my song "Moments, Seconds & Minutes," and had just finished a perfect take.  Just as the note faded out, my daughter walked in the room.  She was about 5 and had a burning question for me! I mixed the song and left off her question, but I saved her comment on the master reel and treasured it for the longest time figuring that when I released the song I would leave the comment intact.  Tragically, the master reel was destroyed in a basement flood.  I have convinced myself that somewhere I have a cassette copy of a rough mix of the song with her question still intact, and I will find it someday. The picture of me playing it in the studio was taken during the recording of my band's first EP. It is true that each of my instruments hold so many wonderful memories for me!
