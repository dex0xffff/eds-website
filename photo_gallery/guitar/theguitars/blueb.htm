Guitar Gallery

 

Company: BlueBird
Model#: Custom
Year: Estimated 1940
Description: This guitar, though not an expensive model, is very special.  It was originally owned by a member of my wife's family on her father's side, named "Uncle Bunk," whose picture is below.  It has some unique features, the most prominent of which is the interesting stain job on the fingerboard, with the dotted frets left unstained.  I have never seen this on any other interument.                               Other nice appointments are the brass tuning keys, the stenciled-on headstock log and decoration, Stenciled rosette, and the stenciled on "pickguard," almost defeating the purpose of a pickguard all together.
