2003 Schedule Highlights

Where Dates & Times
Cederhurst Coffeehouse
Cederhurst Unitarian Church

Friday - February 1 - This was our second time at this venue and we had a great time.  We played first to a large crowd. 

House Concert
 Hosted by Paul Weager

Friday - April 4 - This was our first performance at a "House Concert" and we thoroughly enjoyed performing without the mountain of sound equipment we normally use.  The intimate setting made for a special evening of music unlike we have experienced, even in the Red Door Coffeehouse days!  We were formally joined by friend Steve McCoy.  Our first performance as a trio and we felt right at "HOME!"  Thanks to those who attended!  We will be looking for new "House Concert" venues, so check back  for our next date...  
Diversified Life Coffeehouse
Sponsored by
Anne Arundel Community College

What a great event this turned out to be!  There were about 60 people in attendance and we raised enough money to send two students to "Peer Educators" training, which was the goal of the event.  They even called for an encore!
The Tawes Garden
 Dept. of Natural Resources Building
Annapolis, MD		 This was a "Sparks & McCoy" outing which was threatened by rain, but we ended up with a beautiful Spring day.  We were surrounded by about 80 folks. Thanks to Amy Henry for having us.  
Homestead Gardens

This was the first outing of "Raymond & McCoy" and a great time was had by all.  Check back for future dates for this duo!
B&A Walking/Bike Trail 
 At the Gazebo on the Trail
Severna Park, MD		 What a great day, what a great location, and what a great time we had!  Of the many, many passers by on the B&A Trail that day that stopped to listen, about fifty either arrived with chairs and/or blanket to stake a spot in front of us.  The only down- side of this concert was that we only had an hour slot... we could've played for this crowd all night. 
Music on the Parkway
Weather was perfect this day and we ended up outside on the CPBC's giant stage!  Many people came to picnic and listen...and some just to listen. This was the forth year in a row for Sparks & Raymond, and the first time Steve joined us.  We hope to play this venue in '04, so check back for dates.
Disabilities Awareness Day
 Hosted by A. A. Co. Disabilities Commission
Lake Waterford Park 		Unfortunately, even though "they" were calling for good weather, we got rained out!  Oh well, next year they promise to have a rain date or plan the event somewhere with an "indoors!"  Mostly we felt bad for the people who would have benefited the most, the disabled.  
Homestead Gardens Fall Fest
 www.homesteadgardens.com		 What a great day we had!  Plenty of people showed up and we think most of them gathered in the entertainment tent to listen and sing along!   

All the coffee we could drink AND paid too...what a deal!!!
It was a cold rainy night the day after Thanksgiving but we had a great time playing at Cravings for the first time. There were about 20 people there enjoying the hot coffee ant treats that Cravings had to offer and we played several requests... and even gave away two "Sparks & Raymond" T-shirts.   

Park Plaza Shopping Center
562-B Ritchie Highway, Severna Park, MD
Corporate website: http://www.hardbean.com/
Boy are we drinking alot of coffee these days!!!
We performed to a full house at The Hard Bean for our debut there. Once again we gave away two "Sparks & Raymond" T-shirts.  We hope to become a regular feature at the Hard Bean Check back here soon for an update...
