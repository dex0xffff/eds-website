Patrick Raymond

Acoustic Musician
Guitar Harmonica Mandolin
 
Patrick Raymond has been writing songs and playing music now for over 40 years. Patrick has taken up guitar, mandolin, banjo, dobro, and harmonica. Not only is Patrick a seasoned musician and songwriter, but in 1993 found a love for building instruments. So far he has built 3 steel string guitars, and has 2 classical guitars under construction. His own personal instrument, shown below, has a beautiful abalone/mother of pearl inlay of a trout pursuing a fly. Fishing is another of Patrick's loves! As if this isn't enough to fill his time, he is also a High School Guidance Counselor and a ultra-marathon trail runner.
