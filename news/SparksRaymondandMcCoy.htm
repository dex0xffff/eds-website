Not sure how to get there...
     Sparks, Raymond & McCoy recommend you use MapQuest at the MapQuest web site.  It's really easy!
     1.  Click on the MapQuest logo below -or- the text link below it to get to the MapQuest page to enter
            "Driving Directions.".
     2.  Type in your address on the left.
      3.  Type in the address of the location where Sparks, Raymond & McCoy are performing on the right.
     4.  At the bottom, choose "Get Directions"
     5.  Print out your map (it can even be in color).  Your map will also include text directions and will tell you the
            total miles from your place and the total time to allow to get there!
