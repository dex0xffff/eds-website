Animal Resource Foundation, Inc.
 61 Piney Narrows Road, Chester, MD 21619
(410) 643-8700
 info@arfusa.org    http://www.arfusa.org

Animal Resource Foundation is dedicated to saving and improving the lives of abandoned, abused and neglected companion animals throughout the region.  Since its founding in 1999 ARF has helped thousands of cats and dogs from the danger of euthanasia.  Special medical services are provided for disabled and abused animals.  Each animal receives routine vaccinations, are spayed and neutered prior to adoption and special needs animals are rehabilitated.  Every animal that enters ARF is loved and cared for as if they were our own before they are adopted to carefully selected loving homes.

 Each membership, donation and proceeds from fundraisers the funds are used to help us further our efforts to spay, neuter, off-set medical expenses and to raise awareness to this very special cause.  It also keeps food in the animals bowls and helps maintain our adoption center while the animals are staying with us.

 We also accept wish list items:  Paper towels, hand sanitizer, bleach, dish washing soap, laundry detergent (prefer powder as it last longer) dog/puppy toys, cat/kitten toys, clumping cat litter, dog/cat treats, any kind of  wet dog food, Fancy Feast grilled wet cat food, gift certificates to Bev's Grooming or PetSmart, large size kitchen trash bags, 33 gallon size trash bags, collars and 6' dog leashes.

Not sure how to get there...
 
     Sparks, Raymond & McCoy recommend you use MapQuest at the MapQuest web site.  It's really easy!
     1.  Click on the MapQuest logo below -or- the text link below it to get to the MapQuest page to enter
            "Driving Directions.".
     2.  Type in your address on the left.
      3.  Type in the address of the location where Sparks, Raymond & McCoy are performing on the right.
     4.  At the bottom, choose "Get Directions"
     5.  Print out your map (it can even be in color).  Your map will also include text directions and will tell you the
            total miles from your place and the total time to allow to get there!
