Not sure how to get there...
     Sparks & Raymond recommend you use MapQuest at the MapQuest web site.  It's really easy!
     1.  Go to the MapQuest site and choose "Driving Directions" from the graphic on the page.
     2.  Type in your address on the left and then the address of the location where Sparks & Raymond are
            performing that you need directions to on the right.
     3.  Under "Route Type" choose "Door-to-Door"
     4.  At the bottom, choose "Calculate Directions"
     5.  Print out your map (it can even be in color).  Your map will also include text directions and will tell you the
            total miles from your place and the total time to allow to get there!
