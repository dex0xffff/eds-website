After 23 years together, Free Flight played their last show on
November 27, 2004 at the Cheshire Crab.  Although they will not be
performing as Free Flight, they have by no means retired from music!
Joel McGuire can be seen playing solo from Baltimore to Ocean City,
and will be joined by Steve McCoy on many dates as well!
Edward Sparks will still be playing with his music partner of 15 years,
Patrick Raymond in their duo "Sparks & Raymond,"
and with Patrick and Steve as "Sparks, Raymond & McCoy."  

 

Free Flight would like to thank all of you
who supported us over the years... family, friends, and fans!
