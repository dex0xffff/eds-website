Edward Sparks has been performing for the last 25+ years.  He plays 6 and 12 string acoustic and electric guitars, acoustic and electric bass, harmonica, and a "little banjo and mandolin."

 
He also collects vintage stringed instruments and currently owns a variety of instruments including a Vega "banjolin," a very old hybrid instrument which has the neck of a mandolin attached to a small banjo body.

Besides performing  with Patrick Raymond as "Sparks & Raymond," he performs with Steve McCoy and Joel McGuire as part of the acoustic/electric/electronic trio, called FREE FLIGHT, founded by Sparks some 15 years ago. Their regular set lists include "covers" from Beatles to Pink Floyd, Crosby- Stills-Nash & Young to Genesis, Cat Stevens to the Electric Light Orchestra, James Taylor to Gin Blossoms, with originals as well!

 


Besides performing solo on occasion, Edward is a member of the acoustic duo, "Sparks & Raymond."  He and partner Patrick Raymond operated as well as hosted a monthly non-profit acoustic music venue called "The Red Door Coffeehouse" for almost ten years.  They closed the Red Door in March of 2002 to allow them time to perform more as a duo.  "Sparks & Raymond" present a catalog of original tunes, as well as a wide variety of select cover tunes.
